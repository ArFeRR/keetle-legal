Keetle · kettle boil-timer for iPhone

Keetle times how long your kettle takes to boil — by physics, not guesswork — and shows a live countdown on your Lock Screen, Dynamic Island, and widgets.

Getting started

  • Add your kettle — pick it from the catalog, scan its label or barcode, or enter wattage and capacity by hand.
  • Tap Boil — Keetle estimates the time from your kettle's power, fill level, and starting water temperature.
  • Follow along — watch the countdown in the app, on the Lock Screen, or in the Dynamic Island.

Common questions

The time isn't exact. Keetle shows an approximate time; real kettles vary with scale, voltage, and lid. A one-time calibration boil sharpens future estimates.

Room temperature looks off. Allow location for automatic local weather, or tap the temperature to set it yourself.

Managing your subscription. Premium is billed by Apple — manage or cancel it in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions. A free trial converts automatically unless cancelled before it ends.

Restoring purchases. Use “Restore Purchases” on the paywall to recover an existing subscription on a new device.
