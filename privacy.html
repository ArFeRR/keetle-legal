Privacy Policy

Keetle · Last updated: May 31, 2026

Keetle is a kettle boil-timer for iPhone. We built it to respect your privacy: Keetle has no user accounts, no servers of our own, and no analytics, tracking, or advertising. This policy explains the limited data the app handles and why.

What stays on your device

Your kettles and your boil history are stored only on your device (via Apple's on-device database) and in your own device/iCloud backups. We never receive, see, or store this information. Deleting the app removes it.

Location

With your permission, Keetle uses your approximate location to estimate tap-water and room temperature from local weather, which makes the boil-time estimate more accurate. Weather is provided by Apple Weather (WeatherKit); location-based weather requests are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy terms. We do not store, log, or share your location. Location is optional — you can deny or revoke it in iOS Settings, and the app still works with a default estimate.

Purchases

Keetle Premium is sold through the Apple App Store. Purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple; we receive only your entitlement status (whether Premium is active) — never your name, card, or payment details.

Third-party services

  • Apple Weather (WeatherKit) — for temperature estimates.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — for purchases and subscriptions.

No other third parties receive your data. Bundled open-source components (fonts and animations) collect nothing.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone. There is nothing to sell — your data stays on your device.

Children

Keetle is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone.

Your choices

Because your data lives on your device, you are in control: revoke location access in iOS Settings at any time, and delete all app data by removing the app. Manage or cancel a subscription in the App Store (Settings → your name → Subscriptions).

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the new version here.
